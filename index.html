    ABOUT

    MY GRANDPARENTS

    Moshe and Ranya David were born in southern Yemen. Moshe was born in 1932 and Ranya was born in 1935. They grew up in the same village in Yemen and already knew at an early age that they were going to get married. In 1949, all the Jews in the village decided to immigrate to Israel, Operation "Magic Carpet". In 1950 Moshe and Ranya married in Israel, in the city of Rosh Ha'ayin in a large tent with ten people for "minyan". They live in Moshav Halutz in the south of the country and now live in the city of Ashkelon, they have 7 children and lots of grandchildren and great grandchildren. Moshe is now a cantor in the synagogue, he taught all grandchildren their affair to the Bar Mitzvah and continues to do so with the great-grandchildren. Ranya prepares very tasty traditional Yemeni foods and also shared some recipes with us
